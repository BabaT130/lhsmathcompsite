Lynbrook Math Open

Lynbrook High School's annual math competition.

LMO includes an individual round, a team round, interesting activities, and a full day on campus with other competitors.

Prizes

Team Round

  • 1st place: $100
  • 2nd place: $60
  • 3rd place: $20

Individual Round

  • 1st place: $50
  • 2nd place: $25
  • 3rd place: $10
  • 4th place: $5
  • 5th place: $5

Four $25 AoPS coupons will be randomly raffled to participants. As of right now, all participants will get a share of $1000+ worth of free swag, graciously sponsored by Hudson River Trading.

There will also be physical prizes, including medals, plaques, and certificates.
