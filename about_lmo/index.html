About Lynbrook Math Open

LMO is Lynbrook High School's annual student-run math competition, with an individual round, a team round, and a full day on campus with other competitors.

Competition

The contest includes an individual round and a team round, with difficulty roughly spanning early AMC 10 through mid-to-late AIME.

The day also includes lunch, activities, problem disputes, and a closing ceremony with awards.

Community

LMO is organized by Lynbrook students and supported by volunteers, sponsors, and the wider math community around the competition.

Questions can be sent to lynbrookmath@gmail.com.
