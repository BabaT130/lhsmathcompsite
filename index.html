Lynbrook Math Open

The Lynbrook Math Open (LMO) is an annual math competition run by high school students, with an Individual Round and a Team Round. We invite students across FUHSD to join us for the 2026 competition. Registration is required for everyone who plans to compete.

The contest has two rounds: twelve problems in the individual round and fifteen in the team round. Difficulty spans roughly early AMC 10 through mid-late AIME.
